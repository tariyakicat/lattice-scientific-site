# Blog SEO and AI Search Checklist

## Every deployment

- Run `npm run build` and resolve all Astro content-schema errors.
- Confirm canonical, Open Graph, Twitter, BlogPosting, FAQPage, and BreadcrumbList output.
- Test representative posts in Google Rich Results Test.
- Check that `/rss.xml`, `/sitemap-index.xml`, `/robots.txt`, and `/llms.txt` return successfully.
- Verify new posts appear in the blog index, category page, tag pages, RSS feed, and sitemap.

## Search discovery

- Submit the sitemap to Google Search Console.
- Submit the sitemap to Bing Webmaster Tools. Bing indexing is important for discovery by search products that depend on Bing's index, including some AI-search experiences.
- Consider IndexNow after the publishing cadence justifies it.
- Inspect indexing and canonical reports after important launches.

## Quarterly content refresh

- Re-check journal requirements, prices, specifications, software capabilities, and external links.
- Update `dateModified` only after a meaningful review.
- Replace editorial placeholders with verified copy and primary-source citations.
- Review FAQ questions against current search and client enquiries.
- Remove or clearly date claims that are no longer current.
